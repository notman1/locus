Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Locus 2k26 Tech Fest (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects the personal information of users who register for events through this website.

Information We Collect

During registration, we may collect the following details:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • College / Institution details

For processing payments, transactional information is handled securely by our payment gateway partner Razorpay. We do not store your card, UPI, or banking credentials on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Registering you for selected events
  • Communicating important event-related updates
  • Verifying your identity during participation

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is shared only with:

  • Event organizers and coordinators for management purposes
  • Payment gateway (Razorpay) strictly for payment processing

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Payment Gateway

All online payments are processed by Razorpay. Your use of Razorpay services is also subject to Razorpay’s own terms and privacy policy.
